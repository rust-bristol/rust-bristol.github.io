Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of events do you organize?

We primarily organize technical talks, workshops, and social meetups focused on Rust programming. Events typically run for 2-3 hours in the evening.

How often do you hold events?

We aim to organize events depending on venue availability (most critical part) and speaker availability.

Can we sponsor multiple events?

Yes! We're happy to discuss longer-term sponsorship arrangements. This helps us plan better and provides more consistent exposure for sponsors.

Do you provide receipts for donations?

Yes, all donations through Open Collective come with proper documentation for your records.
